ST. JAMES
THE LESS
Roman Catholic Church
We foster love by example.

As a parish community dedicated to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus, we here at St. James are committed to further His mission. Through His Word, the Liturgy and the Sacraments, through music, evangelization and religious education, and through the intercession of our Blessed Virgin Mary, we deepen our knowledge, our understanding, and most especially our practice of our faith put to action.

Committing ourselves to serving the needs of our people for spiritual and social development, and economic growth, it is by design and intention that we foster love by example.

Join us in doing so.
